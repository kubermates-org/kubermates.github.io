  AI is encountering a major operational obstacle. Traditional IT infrastructure and
  existing skillsets are proving insufficient for the demands of contemporary AI models.

  and delaying the value from significant AI investments. The Skills Gap is Your Hidden

  as infrastructure and talent. This deficit is being exacerbated by massive adoption
  we are seeing across the globe. In the Asia-Pacific region, for instance, 78% of
  respondents are using AI regularly, according to BCG. This usage is only expected
  to increase and drive greater demands on AI systems.'
