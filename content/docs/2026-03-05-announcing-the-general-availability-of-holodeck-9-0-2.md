  9.0 Scaling VMware Cloud Foundation 9.0 Lab Environments using Holodeck 9.0 We’re
  excited to announce the General Availability of Holodeck 9.0.2, the latest maintenance
  release that builds on the robust foundation established by Holodeck 9.0 and 9.0.1.
  This new release delivers enhanced capabilities, improved reliability, and expanded
  Day 2 operations for your nested VMware Cloud Foundation (VCF) environments. A heartfelt
  thank you to our growing community for your continued engagement, feedback, and
  adoption of Holodeck. Your insights drive continuous improvement and help us deliver
  a tool that truly meets your testing and training needs. Holodeck continues to revolutionize
  how teams deploy, test, and understand VCF by providing a self-contained, automated,
  and repeatable solution that eliminates the complexity and resource overhead typically
  associated with nested environments. This maintenance release introduces significant
  enhancements that make Holodeck even more powerful and reliable. VCF 9.0.2.0 Support:
  Deploy the latest VCF 9.0.2.0 environments alongside continued support for VCF 9.0.0.0,
  9.0.1.0, 5.2, 5.2.1, and 5.2.2. Supervisor Deployment in Management Domain: Holodeck
  now supports deploying Supervisor in the management domain via the new -DeploySupervisorMgmtDomain
  parameter, expanding your VMware Kubernetes capabilities. VCF Automation All Apps
  Org Creation: Add VCF Automation All Apps Org as a Day 2 operation, enhancing your
  automation workflows after initial deployment. The new Update-HoloDeckInstance cmdlet
  transforms how you manage Day 2 operations: Replaces the previous -Interactive mode
  with a dedicated cmdlet for post-deployment operations. Supports deploying additional
  clusters in VCF instances. Enables All Apps Org deployment in VCF Automation.'
