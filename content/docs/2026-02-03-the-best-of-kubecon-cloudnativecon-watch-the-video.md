summary: 'Posted on February 3, 2026 by epower We’re excited to launch a new video
  celebrating the energy, people, and community that make KubeCon + CloudNativeCon
  what it is. One of the most powerful things about KubeCon + CloudNativeCon is the
  sheer scale and diversity of voices it brings together. As CNCF project maintainer
  Kingdon Barrett shares in the video, “The best thing about KubeCon + CloudNativeCon
  is the sheer number of projects that are represented here… it’s amazing to get to
  meet people from all over the world with all their diverse experience. ” From maintainers
  to first-time contributors, the conference reflects the truly global nature of cloud
  native. For many attendees, KubeCon + CloudNativeCon is also a deeply personal milestone.
  Dan Kohn Scholarship recipient Unnati Atul Mishra describes finally meeting collaborators
  she’d only known online: “I’m able to connect with like-minded people, few of the
  brightest minds in the cloud native space… and meet contributors I used to connect
  with online, now in person. ” These moments, where digital collaboration becomes
  human connection, are what make the experience unforgettable. That openness is something
  people feel immediately. CNCF Ambassador Michael Uanikehi puts it simply: “You can
  literally walk up to anyone and have a conversation without feeling intimidated…
  everyone is open and willing to talk and express themselves. ” Whether you’re new
  to the community or deeply embedded in it, KubeCon + CloudNativeCon creates space
  to learn, share, and belong. Filmed at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America in
  Atlanta in November 2025, we invite you to experience the magic by watching the
  video. And as we look ahead to the next chapter of the cloud native journey, we
  can’t wait to see the community come together again.'
