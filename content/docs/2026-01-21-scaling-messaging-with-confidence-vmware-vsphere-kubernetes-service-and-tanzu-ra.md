  that is built-in with VMware Cloud Foundation (VCF) , without introducing a new
  operational model or tooling stack. You get Kubernetes where it makes sense, while
  keeping vSphere as the control plane your operators already understand. Operational
  continuity VKS integrates directly with vSphere. Cluster lifecycle, networking,
  storage, and access controls follow the same patterns your infrastructure team already
  uses. No parallel platform to manage. Lower operational overhead You avoid retraining
  teams or adding new management layers. Kubernetes runs inside your existing private
  cloud, reducing both ongoing cost and operational risk. Messaging features that
  matter Tanzu RabbitMQ includes capabilities such as Warm Standby Replication and
  intra-cluster compression. These directly improve availability, recovery behavior,
  and throughput in production environments.'
